Our Curated Wine Selection

At Étoile 67, our sommeliers have carefully selected a range of exceptional French wines that perfectly complement our cuisine. From crisp whites to rich reds and effervescent champagnes, each bottle tells a story of terroir, craftsmanship, and passion.

Champagne

Elegant and celebratory, our Champagne selection features the finest French producers, offering notes of citrus, brioche, and delicate bubbles. Perfect for special occasions or to elevate any meal.

White Wine

Our white wines include crisp Chardonnays and fragrant Sauvignon Blancs from Burgundy and Loire Valley. Expect balanced acidity, floral aromas, and hints of green apple and oak.

Red Wine

Deep, full-bodied reds from Bordeaux and Rhône bring richness and depth to your dining experience. With notes of dark cherry, plum, and spice, these wines pair beautifully with our main courses.
