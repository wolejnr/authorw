Welcome to Étoile 67 – Fine French Dining in Gatineau

Nestled in the heart of Gatineau, Étoile 67 is a French dining experience that celebrates the timeless art of gastronomy. Since opening our doors in 2012, we’ve been committed to bringing authentic French cuisine and elegant hospitality to our community. From our signature dishes like Boeuf Bourguignon and Soupe à l’oignon gratinée to delicate desserts such as Crème brûlée, every plate is crafted with passion and precision.

Located at 325, boul. de la Cité-des-Jeunes, Étoile 67 offers a cozy yet sophisticated atmosphere where guests can savor the finest ingredients and curated French wines. Whether you’re joining us for a romantic evening, a business dinner, or a celebration, our chefs and sommeliers ensure each visit is a memorable culinary journey.

At Étoile 67, dining is more than a meal — it’s an experience that blends tradition, artistry, and exceptional service. We invite you to discover the taste of France, right here in Gatineau.
